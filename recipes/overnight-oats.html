Overnight Oats Recipe

Overnight Oats are an easy and healthy breakfast or snack. With minimal prep you can have delious, protein packed and filling overnight oats with endless topping options.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup whole rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • ½ teaspoon maple syrup, plus more for serving
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • ¼ cup whole milk Greek yogurt, optional
  • ⅔ cup unsweetened coconut or oat milk

Steps

  1. Combine the oats, chia seeds, maple syrup, sea salt, greek yogurt and coconut milk.
  2. Mix well until combined and there are no clumps of chia seeds throughout the mixture.
  3. Add fresh toppings and extra maple syrup right before serving and enjoying!

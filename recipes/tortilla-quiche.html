Tomato Tortilla Quiche

Tortilla Quiche is a quick and easy way to make a healthy and hearty breakfast or snack. Minimal ingredients and prep, throw it in the oven and you're ready to go.

Ingredients

  • 1 8-10 inch tortilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cottage cheese or greek yogurt
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Pepper
  • Diced Tomato
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Line cake or pie tin with tortilla and mix eggs, salt, peper and cottage cheese/greek yogurt together.
  2. Add diced Tomato and sprinkle shredded cheese over the top.
  3. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until set.

Tteokbokki Recipe

Tteokbokki is a Korean dish consisting of rice cakes smothered in spicy sauce. Add an egg or fish cakes for protein and add a pack of instant noodles if you're looking for a more filling meal.

Ingredients

  • Tteok (Rice Cakes)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Gochujang
  • Honey or Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot or pan.
  2. Add to pan 2 Tbsp of Soy Sauce, 1 Tbsp of Garlic, 1 Tbsp of Gochujang, 1 Tbsp of Honey (or Brown Sugar).
  3. Rinse and add frozen Tteok and cook for 5 minutes and 30 seconds.
  4. Add Eggs into pan and cover with for 1 minute and 30 seconds or until they are set.
  5. Garnish with Green Onion and enjoy!
